Our Mission

We believe that everyone has value. Their value can be increased by comparing themselves only to their previous self. In this respect we try to focus on improvement more than outcomes. We believe that there is no better vehicle to focus on self improvement than wrestling. It provides all the challenge and adversity necessary as well as the self-fulfillment that comes with conquering these obstacles! We are excited to help youth to become better people through the sport of wrestling.
